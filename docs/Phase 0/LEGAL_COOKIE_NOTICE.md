## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## 2. HOW TRIPLINK USES COOKIES

### 2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the Platform to function:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `auth_token` | JWT authentication token | Session / 1 hour |
| `session_id` | Session management | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Security (CSRF protection) | Session |

**Cannot be disabled:** These cookies are required for Platform functionality.

---

### 2.2 Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enhance your experience:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `preferences` | User preferences storage | 1 year |
| `language` | Language preference | 1 year |

**Can be disabled:** You can disable these in your browser settings, but some features may not work.

---

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies (If Implemented)

These cookies help us understand how the Platform is used:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `analytics_id` | Usage tracking (anonymized) | 2 years |

**Can be disabled:** You can opt out of analytics tracking.

## 4. MANAGING COOKIES

### 4.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies

### 4.2 Platform Settings

- Cookie preferences can be managed in your account settings (if implemented)
- Some cookies are essential and cannot be disabled
