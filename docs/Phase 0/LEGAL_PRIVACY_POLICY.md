# Privacy Policy

**TripLink Platform**  
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-04  
**Version:** 1.0 (Draft)

---

## 1. INTRODUCTION

TripLink ("we," "our," "Platform") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

**Important:** TripLink is currently an academic/prototype platform. This policy reflects our commitment to privacy-by-design principles, even in a development environment.

---

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email, password (hashed), name, phone
- **Profile Information:** Avatar, travel preferences, interests
- **Travel Data:** Trips, itineraries, bookings, wishlists
- **Content:** Reviews, messages, travel documents (metadata)
- **Agent Applications:** Business information, experience, documents

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, timestamps
- **Device Information:** Browser type, IP address (anonymized)
- **Session Data:** Login sessions, authentication tokens

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Stripe Sandbox:** Payment simulation data (no real payments)
- **Email Service:** Delivery status (for verification emails)

---

## 3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

### 3.1 Platform Operations

- Provide and maintain the Platform
- Authenticate and manage your account
- Process bookings (simulated)
- Enable communication between users and agents

### 3.2 Personalization

- Recommend destinations based on preferences
- Customize your experience
- Show relevant content

### 3.3 Communication

- Send verification emails
- Send password reset emails
- Send in-app notifications
- Respond to support requests

### 3.4 Legal & Compliance

- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce Terms of Service
- Protect Platform security
- Prevent fraud and abuse

---

## 4. DATA SHARING & DISCLOSURE

### 4.1 Public Information

- **Public Profiles:** Name, avatar (if made public)
- **Reviews:** Posted reviews are visible to other users
- **Agent Profiles:** Agent information is visible to travelers

### 4.2 Limited Sharing

- **Agents:** Assigned agents can view your trip and booking information
- **Administrators:** Full access for moderation and support
- **Service Providers:** Stripe (sandbox), email services (for Platform operations only)

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Protect Platform security
- Enforce Terms of Service
- Respond to legal requests
- Prevent harm to users

---

## 5. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

### 5.1 Storage

- Data is stored on secure servers
- Passwords are hashed (never stored in plain text)
- Sensitive data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)

### 5.2 Security Measures

- Authentication via JWT tokens
- Rate limiting on login attempts
- Session management
- Regular security updates

### 5.3 Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while account is active
- **Bookings:** Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Messages:** Retained for 1 year
- **Analytics:** Aggregated data retained for 2 years
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data anonymized or deleted per GDPR

---

## 6. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

### 6.1 Access

- You can request a copy of all your personal data
- Data export available (implementation in Phase 4)

### 6.2 Correction

- You can update your profile information at any time
- You can edit or delete your content (reviews, trips)

### 6.3 Deletion

- You can delete your account at any time
- Some data may be retained for legal/compliance purposes
- Bookings may be archived (not fully deleted) for 7 years

### 6.4 Portability

- You can request your data in machine-readable format
- Data export feature (implementation in Phase 4)

### 6.5 Objection

- You can object to certain data processing
- Contact us to exercise your rights
